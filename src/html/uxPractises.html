Top websites for UI/UX inspiration and guidelines

UX Planet



This is a great little website, one of the best, but of course being the best comes with a price tag. You get to read a couple of articles a months but only a certain amount but we think it is worth paying to get unlimited UX knowledge

Adobe



Adobe have some great resources on their website and seeing as we use adobe XD here UI/UX things then we have to give them a mention.

UX pin



Here is a great article on consistency by UXpin. They have some interesting articles and insightful articles as blog posts where you could learn a lot off of.

Interaction Design Foundation



This website can be a bit more on the technical side so if you are feeling up to the challenge then this can be a very insightful website. They also offer courses on interaction design.

Designer



Designers is a website where you can actually hire UI/UX designers but we think this article is a gem because it focuses on creating a UI style guide for better UX and we think a proper style guide is crucial if you are starting up a website for business, it can make you look proper professional

Intechnic



Well we don't need to say much here only it is 100 tips from a UX master! This article gives some greats insights into how to guide the user around your website using nudges and subtle visual cues



There you have some of our favorites, there are many other blog websites such as medium where you can pick out interesting articles on UI/UX design also but we didn't want to overwhelm you getting you to look through the 1000's of articles they have. Some of the articles linked might seem a little repetitive but just take one at a time and we can assure you will learn something different off each one and from here put your own stamp on your design while following the guidelines.
