    How to on everything usability testing

Welcome to our simple guide on how to do a discount usability test, there is a sample one at the bottom of this page but to show you how easy it is to make one, why not follow the simple tutorial below.



First thing you have to is to make out 10 tasks for the user to do on your website, here are a few examples:
  • Go and search for a product on this page
  • Go to put an item into the cart
  • Go to contact customer support about a return

When deciding your task, you should make these so they suit what a typical user journey would be like on your website, this is dependant on business goals also.
