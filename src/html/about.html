About Us

Our journey started back in September 2019 when our founder and CEO David Twomey got the opportunity to do a research project on a subject he had interest in but related to his field of studies. David had months of work experience as a UI/UX intern and so put his skills he learnt to use to learn more about how young business' starting off could improve the user interface and user experience of their websites. We know that first impressions last but we also know business' have budgets and some may not have a UX team. So here at UI/UX things we can help you quickly improve your website and in turn improve conversion rates a.k.a. money for your business, and for good value of time and money!

We do not have facebook or twitter pages set up yet but in the mean time here is a tasty little page for you to follow where they post useful and interesting posts regularly.
